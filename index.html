ELC Wellness App – Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the ELC Wellness App, created for staff of Empowered Living Choices (ELC), handles information when you use its wellbeing tools and actions.

1. Information We Collect

We process only the text you provide (e.g., “I feel stressed”). If you use an Action (such as boundary scripts), we use the input only to return the requested result.

2. How We Use Information

Inputs are used to generate supportive responses. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

Inputs remain within ChatGPT by default and are not stored by ELC. Sensitive details about clients, HR, or medical info should not be entered.

4. External Services

Some Actions may call external services to fulfil requests. Only the minimum data needed is sent.

5. Your Choices

You may stop using the app at any time. If future versions introduce logs, you will have the right to request deletion.
